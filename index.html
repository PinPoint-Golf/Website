01

Three things, measured at once.

Cameras

Off-the-shelf high-speed cameras — face-on and down-the-line, 150fps and up. ChArUco-calibrated and triangulated into true 3D. The cameras you can buy, not a sealed rig.

Motion

Two or more 200Hz IMUs — readily-available BLE sensors — track wrist flexion, extension and rotation plus the body-segment motion a camera can't see cleanly.

Force

Ground reaction force, estimated from motion, so you can see how the swing loads, shifts and unloads through the strike.

02

Four ways to see a swing.

Swing Analysis

Full-body kinematics from the captured frames: kinematic sequence, X-factor, the timing of how speed builds up the chain.

Wrist Motion

Flexion, extension and rotation across P1–P8 — the wrist story that HackMotion and Dewiz users already look for, in one place.

Ground Force

Loading, weight shift and ground reaction force through the downswing — see where the power comes from, not just where it ends up.

AI Coach

Ask about your swing out loud and get answers grounded in your own numbers — not a generic tip, the one your data supports.
